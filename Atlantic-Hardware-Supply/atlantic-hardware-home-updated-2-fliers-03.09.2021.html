COVID-19 -Recommendations 1. When possible, open outside doors and windows to increase air circulation in the area. When this is not possible as far as cross contamination is concerned, seal off the area and limit the spread. 2. Disinfect wipe down of main touch points (Such as - Door knobs, Handrails, Counters, Keyboards, Mouse, Tables, Desks, Chairs etc). 3. Disinfect wipe down of structural surfaces (Carpets, Hard floors, Walls, Cubicles etc). 4. Disinfect wipe down of all exposed content (Binders, Books, Computers, Screens, Cabinets, Drawers etc). 5. Apply a disinfectant agent to the entire affected area including  the general spaces, cavities, porous materials etc. 6. Items that can be laundered, best treatment is to launder them in accordance with the manufacturer’s instructions using the warmest appropriate water setting for the items.
